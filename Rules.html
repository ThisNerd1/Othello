Rules

        Black always moves first
        Your turn will be over if you can not flip an opposing disc
        You can move horizontally, vertically, and diagonally
        You can not jump over your disc to flip an opposing disc.
        You must flip all caputured discs
        You can not move a disc once it is placed
        Game is over when both players can not move
        The winner is the player with the most pieces on the board
