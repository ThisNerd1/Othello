Rules

  1. Black always moves first.
  2. Your turn will be over if you can not flip an opposing disc
  3. You can move horizontally, vertically, and diagonally
  4. You can not jump over your disc to flip an opposing disc.
  5. You must flip all caputured discs
  6. You can not move a disc once it is placed
  7. Game is over when both players can not move
  8. The winner is the player with the most pieces on the board
